# Name
Two Time Pad

## Description

A disgruntled employee at TechCorp was caught attempting to exfiltrate sensitive company data. During the investigation, we recovered two encrypted images from their personal device that we believe were sent to a competitor. Forensic analysis reveals both images were encrypted using what appears to be a one-time pad encryption scheme, a method that is impossible to break when used correctly.

However, one of our analysts noticed something peculiar: the timestamps on both encrypted files are within minutes of each other. According to the company calendar, the employee had been called into an unexpected meeting with HR that afternoon, and they likely predicted that their access would be revoked. In their haste to send the data before their credentials were deactivated, they may have taken a shortcut somewhere.

Can you determine what sensitive data may have been sent from these two images?
