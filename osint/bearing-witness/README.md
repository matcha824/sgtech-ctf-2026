# Bearing Witness

**Category:** OSINT

## Description

A colleague sent me this photo from a quiet roadside stop during a work trip. They said the location looked unremarkable at first, but it was once part of something much larger.

Can you determine exactly where they were standing and the compass direction the camera was facing?

**Flag format:** `sgctf{site_entrance_identifier_bearing}`

Use lowercase, underscores, and a three-digit bearing.
