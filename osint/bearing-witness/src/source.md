# Source notes

Player-facing image (`../bearing-witness.png`) is a cropped Google Street View
screenshot taken from the ND-5 entrance to Remote Sprint Launch Site No. 3
(RSL-3) of the Stanley R. Mickelsen Safeguard Complex, Cavalier County, North
Dakota. The crop was chosen so the three structures called out in the LoC HAER
caption (operations building / berm tower on left, exclusion-area sentry at
distant center, limited-area sentry on right) are all visible, and EXIF was
stripped before publication.
