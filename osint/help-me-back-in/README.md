# Help me back in

## Description

It's a quiet day at a coffee shop and you notice that Sarah nearby seems to be a little frustrated because she's locked out of her account. It seems like she needs to pay a bill really soon, and she just can't seem to remember her password

Being a social engineer, you take this opportunity to see if you can get details of Sarah's account. Taking the initiative, you somehow get Sarah's number, leave the coffee shop, and call her number from a fake number disguised as tech support for the account she's locked out of. Your job is to converse with Sarah to get some details and hopefully jog her memory about the password.

## Challenge

You are given an executable binary to run. Please pick one that is native to your system from the options. They all run the same data.

The executable starts with dialogue from Sarah and presents you with different options. You select an option you want to pursue and are then prompted with a response from Sarah and more options to continue the conversation.


**Flag format:** `sgctf{PASSWORD}`
